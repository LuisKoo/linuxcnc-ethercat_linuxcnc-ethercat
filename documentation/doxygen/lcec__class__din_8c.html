registers a single digital-input channel and publishes it as a LinuxCNC HAL pin.

Parameters
pdo_entry_regsa pointer to the pdo_entry_regs passed into the device _init function.
slavethe slave, from _init.
idthe pin ID. Used for naming. Should generally start at 0 and increment once per digital in pin.

sindxthe PDO sub-index for the digital input.
